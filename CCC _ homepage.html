The Chaos Computer Club (CCC) is the largest hacker association in Europe and has been a mediator in the area of tension between technical and social developments for over forty years.

His activities range from technical research and exploration on the edge of the technology universe Campaigns, Events, Policy advice, Notices to the operation of anonymization services and means of communication. The club consists of decentralized groups in the German-speaking area, the regular Events and organize meetings.

The CCC communicates its concerns through a wide range of Publications and seeks dialogue with technically and socially interested and like-minded people. It demands and promotes fun on the device and lives the principles of Hacker ethics.
